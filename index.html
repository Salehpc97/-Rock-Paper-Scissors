about
Rock, Paper, Scissors is a simple yet fun hand game played between two people. Here’s how it works:
1-Each player secretly chooses one of three hand gestures:
  • Rock (a closed fist)
  • Paper (a flat hand)
  • Scissors (a fist with the index finger and middle finger extended, forming a V)
2-Players count down together by saying “Rock, Paper, Scissors” while shaking their fists.
3-On "Scissors" (or "Shoot" in some versions), both players reveal their chosen gesture.
4-The winner is determined based on these rules:
  • Rock beats Scissors (Rock crushes Scissors)
  • Scissors beats Paper (Scissors cut Paper)
  • Paper beats Rock (Paper covers Rock)
  • If both players choose the same gesture, it’s a tie, and they play again.
